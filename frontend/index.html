Dice is health, strength, and money

Board is 2 rings, with a “fan” between the rings
• The fan blades function as bridges between the inner and outer rings
• The fan blades move every turn

Players start on the inner ring
Monster starts on the outer ring

Players start with no dice

Die pickups start on all non-wall spaces on outer ring

Win condition: First player to get back to the middle with the trinket and 3 dice wins

On your turn:
  1. Movement: roll 3 dice
    a. Monster moves lowest number, fan moves median number, player moves highest number
      i. You control how all 3 of these things move
    b. Move these 3 in any order, you can also split up movement (e.g. if player moves 3, you can first move 2, next move something else, and then move player 1)
  2. If the player lands exactly on a die, they pick it up
    a. Max 3 dice
      i. If you already have 3 dice, you can’t pick up any more
  3. Dice sacrifices, OPTIONAL: sacrifice dice any number of times to do things
    a. Wall placement: costs 1 die
      i. Roll a die for number of walls to get
      ii. Place new walls OR take control of existing walls owned by other players
      iii. Can only place walls on marked wall spaces
      iv. Only the player who controls a wall may go to the space it is on
    b. Summon monster: costs 1 die
      i. Summon a new monster anywhere on the board, except on top of a player
    c. After sacrificing a die, a die pickup spawns on the farthest away non-wall space on the outer ring
  4. Player vs. Monster combat: If the monster is on the same space as a player, then they must fight; if adjacent to monster, choose whether or not to fight:
    a. Monster rolls 3 dice
    b. Player rolls however many dice they have (0 to 4)
      i. If you have 0 dice you are guaranteed to lose
    c. Highest sum of rolled dice wins fight
      i. Both reroll if tied
    d. If player wins fight:         
      i. Get a trinket
        1. Max 2 trinkets in circulation
    e. If monster wins fight:
      i. Player loses a die
        1. If you already have zero dice before losing one, go back to the middle instead
      ii. Player loses a trinket if they have any
      iii. Monster may attack another player if adjacent
    f. Player decides which adjacent space to go to after fight
  5. Player vs. Player combat: If player lands on same space as another player, they must fight; if adjacent to another player, player who moved chooses whether or not to fight:
    a. Extends rules from player vs. monster
    b. Winning player steals a trinket from the losing player
      i. If the loser doesn’t have a trinket, the winner doesn’t get one
    c. Whoever moved most recently must move away, winning player decides which adjacent space they go to
    d. Losing player loses a die roll’s amount of walls, chosen by the winner
